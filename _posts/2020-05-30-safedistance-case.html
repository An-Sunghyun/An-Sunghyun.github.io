[Labview] Safe Distance Smartphone Case

As the penetration rate of smartphones has increased in modern society, many people walk while looking at smartphones.

As such people experience crashes, the traffic accident rate is on the rise.So I made a smartphone case that could detect danger while looking at Smartphone.

Due to poor conditions, it was not implemented as a physical device and was implemented only in software.

Front Panel

The database basically has member information and CD key information. Through this, members' personal information, scores, and records will be managed, and Java will link them through certain Java classes.

It implements basic community functions such as genuine registration, login, and membership.

Block Diagram

The picture above is block diagram of this program. It has a total of five functions: distance measurement, hazard classification, warning tone output, vibration, and warning statement output.

Functions
  • Distance Measurement
  • Hazard Classification
  • Warning Tone Output
  • Warning Statment Output
  • Vibration function

Each function has been implemented software-only.

Result

Distance measurements were implemented by entering values as if reading sensor values in real time. So it will make person feel as if a person was walking away and getting closer to obstacles.

Depending on the measured distance values, the hazard is classified into four levels of very dangerous, dangerous, careful and safe.

At the very dangerous stage, warning statements and warning tones are output, and LEDs attached to the case repeatedly illuminate to indicate the danger visually and audibly.

At the dangerous stage, a warning statement is printed with vibration to alert the user of the risk.
