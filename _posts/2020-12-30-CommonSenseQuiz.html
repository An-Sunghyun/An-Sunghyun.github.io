[MobileApp] Soft Brain Training

Developed to improve the unorganized nature of quiz applications which have different fields or difficulty levels.

Purpose of project development

  • The purpose is to take both learning and fun.
  • Common sense and many other problems are required regardless of field (except math problems including religion, science, etc.)
  • Used for various purposes, such as playing drinking games or building knowledge
  • Requires leveling, stages, difficulty functions, etc. by borrowing systems from existing puzzle games
  • Need supplementary means, such as hint items
  • All existing games are mixed with too many genres and are not common sense. You can select the genre you want, select intensively, and learn step-by-step from common sense to knowledge and experience.

Problems with existing apps

  • All existing games are mixed with too many genres and are not common sense.
  • UI design is not clear and content is not varied
  • Applications with descriptions often have unnecessarily long explanations
  • Overall, the design of the picture and button is not sincere
  • Quiz-related images often do not express the problem properly.
  • Poor gaming performance due to poor quality and detailed functionality overall (visual/audible effects of correct/wrong answers, buttons and problem designs, etc.)
  • Advertisements are constantly pop-up during game play, hurting the game's of the game.
  • 'common sense' often not a common sense problem

Differentiation and Improvement Plan

  • It does not mix common sense in a wide variety of fields, but it also addresses field-specific issues to focus on learning knowledge about areas of interest.
  • Customized education is provided by dividing the difficulty level without generalization under the name of common sense.
  • Handle truly 'common sense' issues unlike previous applications
  • Select intuitive and familiar designs with maximum care (User-Friendly)
  • Audible/visual effects are provided when certain events occur, such as correct/wrong answers, to facilitate distinguishing.
  • Problem description is divided into two parts, a summary and a detailed description, so that the user can choose.
  • Have ads pop up during the game and be printed on the screen in a small banner at all times to avoid interfering with the game.

Design and Function

Login

  • Account: Log in with the information you entered when you joined the membership.
  • Login function: Link your own account or SNS account when you click Login (implement if possible)
  • Membership registration function: simple human information input and double check with existing accounts in the database
  • Guest login feature: Data is stored only on the device and all data is deleted when the application is deleted.
  • Change button design: When the mouse is raised, the design of the button changes to provide visual focus.
  • Google: Sign in with your Google account (implemented if possible)
  • Other SNS: Log in to the relevant SNS account (implemented if possible)

Register

  • Provide ID, PASSWORD, Nickname and E-mail 4 simple information (edit text)
  • Register in database if ID and nickname are OK after duplicate check

Play Frame

  • Screen to choose from three: Story mode, Challenge mode, and Study mode
  • Story mode: Stage format due to fixed problems (common sense level of difficulty increased by phase)
  • Challenge mode: Select field/difficulty level and time attack to solve problems. Problem questions are random
  • Learning mode: After selecting a field/difficulty level, it proceeds the same as the challenge mode, but not reflected in the ranking

Story Mode

  • Each difficulty consists of a total of 150 sample questions, 50 sample questions.
  • The next stage cannot be challenged unless the previous stage has been cleared
  • Each stage has a fixed problem and cannot be challenged again if it has already been cleared
  • Story mode cannot see the commentary

Challenge Mode

  • Select difficulty/sector
  • Difficulty level is common sense field. Phase 3 of upper, middle, low
  • Common sense problems are 50 by difficulty, a total of 150 questions.
  • In total, there are 10 fields, 20 questions for each field, and 200 questions for each field.
  • Fields: Philosophy, Religion, Psychology, Fourth Industrial Revolution, Computer Engineering, Presentation, Pure Science, Peripheral Instruments, New Cognitive Words)
  • Time limit present (time attack)
  • Time limit of 1 minute per question
  • Fail if you get even one question wrong

Study Mode

Mostly the same as the challenge mode, but with no time limit and can view the commentary
